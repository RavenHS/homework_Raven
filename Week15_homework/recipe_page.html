Kale Scramble

Kale is a nutrient dense superfood that's perfectly fine to eat anytime of the day. We've made it the star of our quick and easy plant based "egg" scramble. This recipe works for both pre and post morning workouts!

The Ingredients

  • 1 cup kale
  • 2 VeganEggs (Follow Your Heart brand recommended)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1 large clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 tbsp vegan butter (Melt brand recommended) or oil

The Recipe

Prep Time: 5 min
Cook Time: 9 min
Servings: 1
  1. Rinse kale of debris and chop into medium sized pieces then set aside
  2. Chop onions and bell peppers and mince garlic
  3. In medium-sized bowl mix 2 tablespoons VeganEgg replacers with 1/2 cup cold water. Whisk to desired consistency
  4. Heat oil or melt vegan butter in pan over medium-low heat. Add garlic and onions and sautee for about 2 mins
  5. Pour VeganEgg mixture into pan and immediately add kale, salt, pepper and desired seasoning. Scramble for about 7 minutes or until eggs are the texture you prefer
  6. Serve with vegan sausage and enjoy!
